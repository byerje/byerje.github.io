Round 1 - Puzzle Round

← Back to Home Page

Solve puzzles as a team to earn clue cards. The more puzzles you solve, the more clues you collect — and the closer you get to solving the mystery!

How It Works

  1. Pick up a puzzle sheet and work together as a team to solve it.
  2. Once solved, send one person to the Key Master (they'll be at a designated station) with the completed puzzle.
  3. The Key Master will give you a QR code to scan. Scanning it adds a clue card to your hand.
  4. Check your clue sheet to see which weapons, suspects, and rooms you can rule out.
  5. Grab the next puzzle and repeat!

Rules

  • There are eight different puzzles to solve.
  • Each team can have one puzzle sheet per puzzle.
  • Teams must stick together and solve the same puzzle until it is complete or abandoned.
  • The next puzzle cannot be started until the whole team is back together.

⏱ Time Limit

Round 1 ends once every room's puzzle has been solved by at least one team — even if your team hasn't finished all of them. Hurry!

Good luck! Be quick and clever to secure your clues and get closer to solving the mystery.
