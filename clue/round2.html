Round 2 - Challenge Round

← Back to Home Page

The puzzles are over — now it's head-to-head! Teams will face off in fast-paced challenges to steal clues from each other.

How It Works

  • Teams will compete head to head in minute-to-win-it challenges.
  • The winning team of each challenge gets to ask the losing team to reveal a clue card (a weapon, suspect, or room) that the winning team doesn't already have.
  • If the losing team has no cards the winning team needs, no card is exchanged — but the win still counts!
  • After a card is revealed, be sure to scan its QR code to add it to your hand.

Use strategy, skill, and quick thinking to win challenges and uncover the mystery!

🏆 Making a Final Accusation

When your team believes you've figured out all three parts of the mystery — the weapon, the suspect, and the room — you can make a final accusation.

  • A final accusation can be made at any time during Round 2.
  • The accusation must be announced in front of the entire group.
  • If any other team holds a card that disproves part of the accusation, they must show that card to the accusing team. The accusing team is then eliminated and cannot guess again.
  • If no one can disprove the accusation, the solutions envelope is opened to check the answer.

Final Rewards

  • There is a prize for the team who makes the correct final accusation.
