Winning the game

A team who believes they have discovered the weapon, suspect, room and motive (all 4) can make a final accusation.

  • A final accusation can be made at anytime.
  • The final accusation must be made in front of the entire group.
  • If someone can disprove the accusation, they will show them one of the clues that disproves it.
  • If no one can prove the accusation false, then the solutions envelope can be opened.

Final Rewards

  • There is a prize for the team who makes the correct final accusation.
