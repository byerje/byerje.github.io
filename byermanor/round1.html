Round 1

← Back to Home Page

The goal of this round is to solve puzzles hidden throughout the Byer manor to earn clues. Players will explore eight guest rooms, each containing a specific type of puzzle to challenge their wit and teamwork. Once a puzzle is solved, show it to the Key Master and your team will be rewarded with valuable clues that will help unravel the mystery. Hurry before time runs out! Quick thinking, strategy, and cooperation are essential. Stay focused, work together, and navigate the twists and turns of the manor to edge closer to solving the murder!

Rules

  • There are eight rooms, each with one type of puzzle to solve.
  • Each team can have one puzzle sheet per room (no more).
  • Teams must stick together and solve the same puzzle.
  • For the metal disentanglement puzzles, if you are familiar with one, choose one you haven’t solved before, to make it fair for everyone. Take a picture of the completed puzzle and then put it back how you found it. Then show the picture you took to the Key Master.
  • Each time you solve a puzzle, send one person down to the Key Master with the solved puzzle to scan a clue.
  • Scanning a clue's QR code with your phone adds it to your clue sheet, or "hand".
  • Look at your clue sheet to see (1)all the cards that have been checked off your list and (2) all discovered motives.
  • The next puzzle cannot be started until both people are back together and ready to move to the next room.
  • If you run into other teams, you must do your assigned interactions and hear theirs.
  • Round 1 will end after every room's puzzle has been solved at least once by a team, even if your team has not completed all the puzzles in all the rooms.
  • The team who finds the most clues will be rewarded.
  • A reward will also be given for a team that finds the 5 tokens AND figurine of their color.

Good luck! Be quick and clever to secure your clues and get closer to solving the mystery.
