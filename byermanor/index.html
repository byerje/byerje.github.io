Important Information:

We are really excited to have another Murder Mystery Dinner Party with you all at the Byer Manor. It's going to be such a fun event!

  • Characters

    Characters are assigned randomly, click the link below to view what characters you are playing. Please note that the important part of the costume is the color, the rest is just a suggestion.

    View Characters

  • Dinner and Sides

    The main course will be provided.

    The rest of the meal is potluck and up to you.

    • Please sign up for and bring a side dish or dessert. Click the link below for the sign up sheet.
    • Please sign up for and bring a drink to share.
    • Be creative and try to make the items you bring match the same color as your character.
    • For example:
      • Mr. & Mrs. Greens could bring a Green Salad and Sprite (in a green can)
      • Lord and Lady Peacock could bring a cheesecake with blueberry topping and blue Powerade.
    Contact your hosts if you get stuck or frustrated and can't think of anything.

    Signup Sheet

  • Pre-game Information

    Follow the link below for storyline information you can read before we play the game. This will introduce you to the plot behind the murder mystery.

    View Pre-Game Details

  • Arrival

    At check-in, you will receive a sealed envelope with 2 QR code cards:

    • one card is your motive
    • one card is a clue card
    Scan them both to add them to your "hand".

  • Cancellation

    If for any reason you can't make it and need to cancel, please let us know as soon as possible. Thanks!

    We completely understand that life happens, and we just want to make sure the party is as fun and full as possible!

  • What to bring?

    - You will need a phone with a camera to play the game (to scan QR codes).
    - Food items: side dish, or dessert, and drinks (thank you for helping with the food).
    - Reading glasses, if you need them.

Thank you so much for joining us!
