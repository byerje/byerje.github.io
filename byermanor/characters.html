Ms. and Mr. Scarlet (Eric and Jen Smith)

Ms. Scarlet’s dramatic flair hides her history of heated arguments with the victim, and Mr. Scarlet’s scripts often feature eerily accurate murder plots.

Ms. Scarlet wears a bold red cocktail dress, red lipstick, and stilettos.

Mr. Crimson Scarlet wears a red velvet blazer, black shirt, and dark trousers.

Lord and Lady Peacock (Matt and Traci Stayner)

Lady Peacock’s penchant for gossip means she knows everyone’s darkest secrets, and Lord Peacock’s military record had a classified section he refuses to discuss.

Lady Peacock wears a royal-blue satin dress, pearl necklace, and a feathered fascinator.

Lord Peacock wears a dinner jacket with a blue hankerchief in his coat pocket.

Mr. and Mrs. Green (Scott and Kelli Broadbent)

Mr. Green's business empire has been under investigation for fraud, and Mrs. Green has been seen whispering to people who mysteriously disappear.

Mr. Green wears a green three-piece suit with a pocket watch and shiny black shoes.

Mrs. Emerald Green wears an elegant emerald-green evening gown with a diamond necklace and long gloves.

Madam and Colonel Mustard (Tony and Annette Barney)

Madam Mustard's globetrotting has left her with enemies across the globe and Colonel Mustard seems to know far too much about the victim’s private life.

Madam Saffron Mustard wears a mustard-yellow safari jacket, wide-brimmed hat, and knee-high boots.

Colonel Mustard wears a bright safari explorer outfit with a leather satchel and binoculars.

Mrs. and Dr. White (Erik and Melissa Cantwell)

Mrs. White was seen arguing with the victim over unpaid wages, and Dr. White’s medical practice lost its license after a patient’s untimely death.

Mrs. White wears a crisp white apron over a black dress with a feather duster in hand.

Dr. Ivory White wears a white lab coat, glasses, and a pocket full of mysterious vials.

Professor and Lady Plum (Marques and Beckie Varela)

Professor Plum’s inventions often malfunction in dangerous ways, and Lady Plum’s collection of rare books includes several detailing infamous poisonings.

Professor Plum wears a purple vest over a white shirt, purple bowtie, and glasses.

Lady Amethyst Plum wears a floor-length amethyst-purple dress with a fur stole and long earrings.

Inspector and Madam Noir (Mason and Eme Rudarmel)

Inspector Noir is rumored to have tampered with evidence in past cases, and Madam Noir’s career as a journalist has involved several mysterious disappearances.

Inspector Noir wears a black trench coat, black fedora, and leather gloves.

Madam Onyx Noir wears a sleek black dress with a wide-brimmed hat and dark sunglasses.

Mr. and Madam Mahogany (Brian and Jamilah Decker)

Mr. Mahogany has been caught selling fake artifacts, and Madam Mahogany’s appraisals have a tendency to undervalue items that later go missing.

Mr. Mahogany wears a brown leather jacket, beige scarf, and a satchel filled with “artifacts.”

Madam Mahogany wears a chocolate-brown dress with gold accents and vintage jewelry.

Dr. and Professor Orchid (Jason and Wendy Quigley)

Dr. Orchid’s experiments in exotic poisons were banned after a deadly accident, and Professor Orchid’s research grants have suspiciously disappeared.

Dr. Orchid wears a pale pink button-up shirt, tweed jacket, and a magnifying glass in hand.

Professor Rose Orchid wears a pink lab coat over a floral blouse, paired with gardening gloves and boots.

Ms. Body met a tragic and mysterious demise that left the community shaken.
