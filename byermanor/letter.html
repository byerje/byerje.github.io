Subject: Your Invitation to the Byer Manor

From: Inspector Noir

Date: April 18, 2025

Dear Guests,

You have been summoned to the Byer Manor on April 18th for reasons that go beyond mere social pleasantries. As you may know, Ms. Body, was found tragically deceased in the Manor's foyer. Her death was untimely, suspicious, and shrouded in mystery.

Each of you has been linked to Ms. Body in one way or another—through business, friendship, or perhaps deeper, darker connections. This gathering is no coincidence. Your presence is required because you are among the key suspects in unraveling this enigma.

As we dine and deliberate, it is my duty to uncover the truth. Know this: no one leaves until the mystery is solved.

I look forward to your cooperation.

Sincerely,

Inspector Noir
