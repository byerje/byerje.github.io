Important Information:

We are really excited to have another Murder Mystery Dinner Party with you all at the Hines Mansion. It's going to be such a fun event!

  • Character and Room Assignments

    Characters and rooms have been assigned randomly, click the link below to view what characters you are playing and which rooms you are staying in. Please note that the important part of the costume is the color, the rest is just a suggestion.

    View Assignments

  • Pre-game Information

    Information you need to read before we play the game. This will introduce you to the plot behind the murder mystery.

    View Pre-Game Details

  • Dinner and Sides

    Dinner will be catered from Mo'Bettas, and will include: Kalua Pig, Chicken (optional teriyaki sauce) and Rice.

    We ask that you bring a side dish or dessert and drinks to share - Be creative and try to make the items you bring the same color as your character. You could match your character's color to the food/drink or the container/can.

    Signup Sheet

  • Arrival and Check In

    Please arrive between 5:00 and 6:00 pm. This will give you time to explore the rooms and get the layout of the hotel in your mind before dinner starts.
    Do not unpack your suitcase yet, since people will be walking through all the rooms during round 1.
    At check-in, you will receive a sealed envelope with 2 QR code cards, one is your motive and the other is a clue card (keep them secret). Scan the motive card first, then the clue card, to add them to your "hand".

  • Breakfast and Checkout

    We will be providing pancakes and other breakfast food items in the morning starting at 8:00 am.

    The hotel provides continental breakfast items (yogurt, granola bars, hot chocolate, etc.), these are availabe in the kitchen either on the counter or in the fridge. Feel free to help yourself.

    Checkout is no later than 11:00 am

  • Cancellation

    If for any reason you can't stay the night, or need to cancel, please let us know as soon as possible so we can invite a couple who can fully attend the event.

    We completely understand that life happens, and we just want to make sure the party is as fun and full as possible!

  • What to bring?

    • You will need a phone with a camera to play the game (to scan QR codes).
    • It is an overnight stay, so bring clothes, toothbrush, etc.
    • Food items: side dish and drinks (thank you for helping with the food).
    • Reading glasses, if you need them.

Thank you so much for joining us!
